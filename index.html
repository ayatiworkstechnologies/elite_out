FOOTBALL TRAINING
Elite Sports Academy

Physical And Mental Ability Enhances A Child’s Early Growth

Understanding the long gap of generation which had not experienced the value of sports academy in a school campus due to space constraints in city limits. We thought of filling this gap by giving our children the opportunity for them to explore their talents in sports and make them physically fit and healthy. This evoked us to dedicate a stretch of 6000 Sq. ft. of carpet area for the sports academy comprising a Football Turf and a wide area for physically trimming our children with different kinds of Martial arts to be taught by well experienced trainers.
